Quizotics

26 May 1 Member
Time Limit:

Each question will have time limit of 10-15 seconds according to the difficulty level of questions.

Question type:

Question type: You will be given 25 questions and each question will have certain time limits. The questions will be multiple choice types.

Description

Participants will be provided with the option to choose any one of the five given topics:

  • Movie robots quiz
  • AI ML
  • IoT and embedded systems
  • Sensors
  • Miscellaneous (25 questions from all above topics)

Participants will have to choose the topic during registration process. The quiz link of the topic they choose will be mailed when the event starts and they have to finish the quiz within 15 minutes. During registration participants have to enter their user name as FullName_School/CollegeName and the same user name they have to enter at the time of giving quiz.

Result:

It will be based on how fast and accurate participants answer the questions.

Rules:
  • Participants have to finish the quiz within 15 minutes after the quiz link mailed.
  • The username will be FullName_School/collegeName. You have to write the same during registration and at the time of quiz.
  • The decision of the judges shall be final and binding.
  • n case of any on the spot discrepancy, the decision of the organizers will be considered final.
